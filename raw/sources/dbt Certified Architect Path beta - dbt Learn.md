	#### Getting Started with git Branching Strategies and dbt
	Elective
	![](https://media.thoughtindustries.com/thoughtindustries/image/upload/a_exif,c_fit,w_440,h_200/v1/course-uploads/6ed554fb-fedc-4719-a40d-8b22e0a22aee/r6dnq7vzw0gi-gitbranchingstrategies.jpg)
	##### Article
	This article offers guidance on utilizing Git branching strategies with dbt for data development workflows, highlighting the significance of source control for team collaboration and managing code changes. It details two main strategies: Direct Promotion, which uses a single main branch, and Indirect Promotion, which incorporates additional branches like QA. The article also covers dbt configurations, data platform organization, and provides examples for both strategies, with considerations for hotfixes and using multiple middle branches.

	#### Securing your account through SSO & RBAC
	Elective
	![](https://media.thoughtindustries.com/thoughtindustries/image/upload/a_exif,c_fit,w_440,h_200/v1/course-uploads/6ed554fb-fedc-4719-a40d-8b22e0a22aee/10peeewv7tqx-2024-05-20-master-titlesslidesforondemandcourses-20251.jpg)
	##### Article
	This article emphasizes the critical step for dbt administrators upgrading to the Enterprise plan: securing their accounts through Single Sign-On (SSO) and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). It explains that implementing SSO and RBAC is foundational for maintaining secure data pipelines, streamlining the process of adding new users to dbt Cloud, and achieving long-term cost efficiency. The article guides readers through configuring SSO with various Identity Providers and managing user authorization using licenses and groups, including the benefits of SCIM for automating user provisioning.
