## About this talk

Discover the power of Dremio, a robust Data Lakehouse platform and query engine, combined with DuckDB, an in-process database, to revolutionize your data analytics experience. By seamlessly integrating these cutting-edge technologies, you can unlock cost savings, scalability, and accessibility, making analytics a breeze for end users. During this informative video, you will: - Explore the capabilities of Dremio and understand how it enables running big data workloads affordably and at scale on your data lakehouse. - Learn about DuckDB and its ability to vastly expand the possibilities of what can be achieved on your local hardware, enhancing performance and efficiency. - Discover how the synergy between Dremio and DuckDB can deliver unparalleled benefits to your data analytics workflows. - Gain insights into how these technologies complement each other, amplifying their individual strengths and providing a comprehensive solution for your analytical needs. - Dive into practical examples and use cases to see how Dremio and DuckDB can be easily leveraged in Python, empowering you to extract valuable insights from your data effortlessly. You will acquire a deeper understanding of Dremio and DuckDB, their unique features, and how they work together harmoniously. You'll be equipped with the knowledge to drive cost-effective analytics, optimize performance, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.
