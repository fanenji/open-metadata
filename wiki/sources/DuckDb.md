# DuckDb Resource Collection

A curated collection of official resources for DuckDB, including the MotherDuck blog, the DuckDB Spatial Extension documentation, the DuckDB Performance Guide, and a video playlist on spatial data management with DuckDB.

## Resources

- **MotherDuck Blog** — [https://motherduck.com/blog](https://motherduck.com/blog) — Official blog of DuckDB's cloud platform, covering best practices, use cases, and product updates.
- **DuckDB Spatial Extension Documentation** — [https://duckdb.org/docs/extensions/spatial](https://duckdb.org/docs/extensions/spatial) — Official documentation for DuckDB's geospatial capabilities, including geometry types, spatial functions, and indexing.
- **DuckDB Performance Guide** — [https://duckdb.org/docs/guides/performance/overview](https://duckdb.org/docs/guides/performance/overview) — Official guide to optimizing DuckDB query performance, covering configuration, indexing, and query tuning.
- **Spatial Data Management with DuckDB Video Playlist** — [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAxJ4-o7ZoPe9SkgnophygyLjTDBzIEbi](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAxJ4-o7ZoPe9SkgnophygyLjTDBzIEbi) — Educational video series on using DuckDB for spatial data management.
