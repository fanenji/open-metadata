# Building a Semantic Layer for the AI Era: Beyond SQL Generation

An article by Mahdi Karabiben arguing that the semantic layer must evolve from a technical tool for SQL generation (the "How") to a context-rich repository of business intent (the "What, Why, and Who") to support the era of AI agents.

The author posits that while the 2022-era semantic layer focused on metric consistency for BI dashboards, the 2025+ era requires a layer that provides the business context necessary for autonomous agents to perform reliable actions.

Key components of the proposed "New Paradigm" include:
- **The How**: Technical calculation (SQL/math).
- **The What**: Business definition and meaning.
- **The Why**: Business purpose and downstream impact.
- **The Who**: Ownership and stakeholders.

The article suggests that a **Knowledge Graph** could serve as the architectural implementation for this centralized, context-rich layer.
