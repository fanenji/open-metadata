# Spatial Data Management course

A public GitHub repository for **GEOG-414 (Spatial Data Management)** at the University of Tennessee, authored and maintained by [[giswqs]] (Qiusheng Wu). The course covers modern geospatial data management techniques including storage, querying, analysis, and visualization of spatial data.

The repository delivers the entire curriculum as code: lecture slides, Jupyter notebooks, assignments, and datasets are all version-controlled and publicly accessible. Topics likely include [[postgis]], [[duckdb-spatial-extension]], [[geoarrow]], [[geoparquet]], [[iceberg-geospatial-support]], and cloud-native geospatial workflows.

This resource complements the wiki's existing coverage of geospatial education (e.g., [[modern-gis-workshop]]) and provides a structured, university-level learning path for spatial data management.
