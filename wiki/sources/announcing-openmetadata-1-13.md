# Announcing OpenMetadata 1.13

Release announcement for OpenMetadata 1.13, focusing on the transition from standardizing metadata to "standardizing meaning" through a semantic context layer.

Key features include:
- **Knowledge Graph**: Unifying technical and business metadata.
- **Ontology Explorer**: Visualizing business ontology.
- **Glossary Terms & Relations**: Typed, RDF-compatible relationships.
- **Columns as Assets**: Treating columns as first-class discoverable entities.
- **New Connectors**: Support for BurstIQ, SSRS, Google Pub/Sub, Matillion, and expanded MCP support.
