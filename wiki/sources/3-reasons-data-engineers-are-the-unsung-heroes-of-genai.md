# 3 Reasons Data Engineers Are the Unsung Heroes of GenAI

As organizations add AI to their products, data engineers will be integral to expanding infrastructure and governance to include new models and technology.

The article argues that while Generative AI (GenAI) captures executive attention, the success of these models depends on the underlying infrastructure and data quality managed by data engineers.

## Key Arguments
1. **Facilitating RAG:** Data engineers build the pipelines that feed accurate, updated, and optimized data into [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] systems.
2. **Security & Governance:** Engineers must extend existing privacy and compliance controls (e.g., access management) to new AI-driven workflows.
3. **Data Quality & Reliability:** Using [[data-observability]] and [[data-lineage]], engineers prevent "garbage in, garbage out" scenarios, such as model hallucinations.
