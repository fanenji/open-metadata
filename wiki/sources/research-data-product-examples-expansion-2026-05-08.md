# Research: Data Product Examples Expansion

A deep research synthesis exploring the expanding landscape of data products beyond traditional analytical datasets. The document surveys 15 external sources to catalog seven categories of data products, clarify the distinction between "data as a product" and "data product," and examine the evolution of the data catalog into an enterprise context layer for AI agents.

## Key Contributions

- **Seven-category taxonomy** of data products: analytical datasets, dbt models, ML features, streaming/real-time, GenAI/vector, API/service-based, and domain-specific operational products.
- **Conceptual clarification** of the "data as a product" mindset versus the concrete "data product" output.
- **Enterprise context layer** concept: the data catalog evolving into the infrastructure AI agents query for authoritative, governed data products.
- **Tension analysis** between universal data products and domain ownership in data mesh.
