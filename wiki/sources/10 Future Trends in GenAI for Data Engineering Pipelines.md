## Key Concepts

### AI-Native Data Pipelines
* **Definition**: Pipelines that use GenAI to infer schemas and structures directly from raw, unstructured data, replacing traditional, rigid ETL.
* **Importance**: Reduces manual preprocessing and maintenance overhead.
* **Wiki Status**: New concept.

### Agentic Orchestration
* **Definition**: The evolution of workflow management where AI agents autonomously manage tasks, reroute failures, and optimize resources.
* **Importance**: Increases pipeline resilience and reduces manual intervention.
* **Wiki Status**: New concept.

### Zero-Prep Data Consumption
* **Definition**: Using self-supervised AI to train models directly on uncleaned, raw data.
* **Importance**: Accelerates model deployment by eliminating the data cleaning bottleneck.
* **Wiki Status**: New concept.

### Federated Learning
* **Definition**: A method of training AI models across distributed datasets without moving the raw data from its original location.
* **Importance**: Essential for privacy-preserving pipelines in regulated industries (Healthcare/Finance).
* **Wiki Status**: New concept.
