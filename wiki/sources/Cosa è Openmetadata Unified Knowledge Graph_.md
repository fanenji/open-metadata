# Cosa è Openmetadata Unified Knowledge Graph_

This source explores the architecture and capabilities of the OpenMetadata Unified Knowledge Graph. It details how OpenMetadata uses a graph-based approach to unify metadata from diverse sources, providing a single source of truth for discovery, observability, and governance.

Key topics include:
- The **4-component architecture** (API, Ingestion, Search, and UI).
- **Pull-based ingestion** mechanisms using 100+ connectors.
- **Metadata Federation**, allowing OpenMetadata to act as a "single pane of glass" by integrating existing catalogs like DataHub, Alation, and Collibra.
- The use of a **schema-first architecture** to support scalability and customization.
- The role of the knowledge graph in providing lineage, ownership, and relationship context.
