# AI-Ready Data vs. Analytics-Ready Data

A conceptual article by Animesh Kumar published on Modern Data 101 (April 2026) that argues analytics-ready data and AI-ready data are fundamentally different, optimized for different consumers (humans vs. machines) with different failure modes. The article introduces the [[analytics-x-ai-maturity-matrix]], a 2x2 framework mapping organizational maturity across two orthogonal axes, and warns against the [[dashboard-trap]] where high analytics maturity creates a false sense of AI readiness. It proposes [[independent-data-maturity-paths]] for the two systems, advocating for coordination rather than convergence.
