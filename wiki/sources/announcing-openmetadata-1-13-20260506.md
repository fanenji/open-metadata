# Announcing OpenMetadata 1.13

Release announcement for OpenMetadata 1.13, focusing on the introduction of a **Semantic Context Layer**. Key features include the **Knowledge Graph**, **Ontology Explorer**, and **Glossary Terms & Relations**, all designed to provide the structured meaning necessary for AI agents to interact with data landscapes reliably.
