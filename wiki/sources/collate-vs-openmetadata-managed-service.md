# Collate vs OpenMetadata Managed Service for Data Teams at Scale

A comparison between the open-source OpenMetadata engine and Collate's managed enterprise service. The document highlights the value proposition of reducing DevOps overhead through SaaS and BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud) models, and details advanced AI-driven features like **Collate AI** (AskCollate, AI Studio, AutoPilot) and **Reverse Metadata** synchronization.
