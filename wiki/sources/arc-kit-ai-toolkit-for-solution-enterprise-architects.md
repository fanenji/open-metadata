# ArcKit — AI Toolkit for Solution & Enterprise Architects

An article by David R. Oliver describing **ArcKit**, an open-source, AI-based toolkit designed to automate the generation of architectural governance artifacts using slash commands within AI coding environments like Claude Code.

The toolkit focuses on reducing the "friction of governance" by generating structured first drafts of ADRs, requirements, and compliance assessments, thereby enabling **knowledge compounding** through machine-readable Markdown files.
