# Manage Teams and Users - OpenMetadata Documentation

Official OpenMetadata v1.12.x documentation page covering the management of teams and users. This page serves as the entry point to the administrative guide for organizational hierarchy setup.

## Key Points

- OpenMetadata provides a **versatile hierarchical team structure** that can mirror an organization's actual setup.
- Admins can create various **team types** to represent different organizational units.
- Users are onboarded into relevant teams.
- Organizations can have **multiple Admins**, enabling decentralized management where different teams and departments are managed by separate Administrators.
- The page links to the **Advanced Guide for Roles and Policies**, indicating that team structure is the foundation for role-based access control.
