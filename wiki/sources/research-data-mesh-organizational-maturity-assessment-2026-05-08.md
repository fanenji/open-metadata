# Research: Data Mesh Organizational Maturity Assessment

A deep research synthesis evaluating organizational readiness and progress in adopting a [[data-mesh]] architecture. This document synthesizes frameworks from multiple consulting firms, cloud providers, and practitioners to define a structured maturity assessment model.

## Key Contributions

- **Synthesized Five-Phase Maturity Model:** Defines a progression from Monolithic (Phase 0) through Foundation, Formalization, Scale, and Optimization (Phase 4).
- **Multidimensional Assessment Framework:** Evaluates organizations across domain ownership, data product thinking, self-serve platform maturity, federated governance, and organizational culture.
- **Jagged Maturity Profiles:** Introduces the concept that organizations may be mature in one dimension but immature in another, challenging linear progression assumptions.
- **KPI Catalog:** Provides measurable indicators for each maturity dimension.
- **Challenges and Contradictions:** Documents known pitfalls, including the single-score vs. multidimensional tension, speed vs. stability balance, and tool vs. culture debate.
