# How to Use Domains | OpenMetadata Governance Guide

This official OpenMetadata documentation page provides a step-by-step guide for adding and configuring Domains in OpenMetadata v1.12.x. It covers the six-step workflow: naming and describing a domain, selecting a domain type (Source-aligned, Aggregate, Consumer-aligned), assigning owners/experts, adding data assets, and filtering assets by domain on the Explore page. The guide positions Domains as a governance tool for organizing data assets by business area or purpose, and it references Data Products as the next logical step in the governance workflow.
