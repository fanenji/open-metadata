# Camp to Camp

Camp to Camp is the organization that employs Florent Gravin and Olivia Guyot, the presenters of the FOSS4GE 2024 talk "Towards Better Data Platforms with Semantic Metadata." They are active in the geospatial open-source ecosystem, particularly around [[geonetwork]] and semantic metadata standards.
