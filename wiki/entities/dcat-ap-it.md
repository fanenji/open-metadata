# DCAT-AP_IT

DCAT-AP_IT is the Italian application profile of the DCAT (Data Catalog Vocabulary) standard for describing public sector datasets in data catalogs. It is the mandatory metadata standard for non-spatial HVD datasets. For spatial data, the RNDT standard applies instead. DCAT-AP_IT ensures interoperability and discoverability of Italian open data across European data portals.
