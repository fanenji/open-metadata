# Foursquare

A geospatial platform company that transitioned from a consumer app to a geospatial data provider. Foursquare's CTO [[Vikram Gundeti]] shared their adoption story on the livestream, describing how they migrated from custom folder structures and JSON formats to Iceberg tables. Foursquare processes billions of impressions, GPS pings, and place data inputs daily. They have open-sourced their Places dataset and use Iceberg for flexible querying, schema evolution, and GDPR compliance.
