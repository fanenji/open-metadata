# Jean-Georges Perrin

Author and creator of the Bitol REST-based data contract management service and the Open Data Contract Standard (ODCS). Published the tutorial "Experimenting with Data Contracts" on Data Mesh Learning (Medium), demonstrating practical DDL-to-contract conversion via API.

Perrin is a data mesh advocate and practitioner, with a focus on making data contracts accessible through simple, API-first tooling. He maintains the Bitol cloud service on JGP.ai and the associated GitHub repository for tutorials and reference material.

## Key Contributions

- Creator of Bitol, a REST-based service for building, validating, and storing data contracts.
- Author of the Open Data Contract Standard (ODCS) specification.
- Published a four-part tutorial series on data contracts and data products.
- Advocate for open standards and practical, hands-on experimentation in data governance.
