# Hugo Lu

Founder of [[Orchestra]], a data release pipeline tool. Author of a miniseries on CI/CD for data teams, including the article "Principles of effective data delivery: How CI/CD should look for Data teams (Miniseries Part 3)". His work adapts the Accelerate framework for data teams and introduces the [[data-ci-cd-principles]] and [[data-release-pipeline]] concepts.
