# Jamie Quint

Jamie Quint (@jamiequint) is a data engineering practitioner and consultant who built the initial data stack at Notion in 2020. He is the author of the guide "How to Build a Data Agent in 2026," which describes a practical approach to building AI data agents that replace traditional semantic layers with dynamic context management.

## Key Contributions

- Pioneered the concept of **context management** as a replacement for semantic layers in the AI Data Stack
- Developed the **quirk knowledge store** pattern for capturing and reusing user corrections
- Designed the **self-scoring agent** mechanism for automated SQL quality assessment
- Demonstrated a 3-week build timeline for a production AI data agent system

## Background

Quint's experience at Notion during the Modern Data Stack era (2020) gives him credibility as a practitioner who has witnessed the evolution from traditional data infrastructure to AI-driven approaches. His consulting work focuses on helping companies transition to the AI Data Stack.
