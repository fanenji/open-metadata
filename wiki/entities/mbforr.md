GitHub user and creator of the [[Modern Gis Workshop]] repository, presented at the I-Guide Forum 2023 at Columbia University. The workshop covers modern geospatial technologies including [[GeoParquet]], [[PostGIS]], [[DuckDB]], [[dbt]], and [[h3-geospatial-indexing|H3]].
