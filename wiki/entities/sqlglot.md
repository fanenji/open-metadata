# SQLGlot

**SQLGlot** is a SQL parser and transpiler integrated into OpenMetadata 1.12 to enhance the performance and capability of lineage parsing.

### Role in OpenMetadata
- **Faster Lineage Parsing**: Dramatically speeds up query parsing for complex lineages containing thousands of nodes.
- **Lineage Accuracy**: Improves the ability to reconstruct complex data flows by accurately parsing various SQL dialects.
