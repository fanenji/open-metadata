# Coalesce Conference

Coalesce is the annual conference organized by [[dbt Labs]] that features presentations from dbt users and use cases across the community. The conference covers dbt best practices, advanced techniques, and the evolving data landscape. It serves as a key resource for learning about dbt adoption patterns and community innovations.
