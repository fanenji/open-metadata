# GPT-4

**GPT-4** is a Large Language Model (LLM) developed by **OpenAI**. In the benchmark study, it served as the primary engine for generating both Text-to-SQL and Text-to-SPARQL queries to evaluate the effectiveness of the semantic context layer.
