# DataForge

A commercial platform promoted in the article "Secrets of the Semantic Layer in Big Tech" as a tool that helps implement the same semantic layer principles used by Uber, Airbnb, and LinkedIn in a form accessible to ordinary data teams. DataForge allows teams to design a centralized semantic model of facts, dimensions, and business metrics.

**Note**: The article's promotion of DataForge introduces potential vendor bias. The platform should be evaluated critically against the architectural patterns documented from Big Tech.
