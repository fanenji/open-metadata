# Carol Ohms

Carol Ohms is a Resident Architect at [[dbt Labs]]. She collaborates with a broad spectrum of customers ranging from startups to enterprises, helping them implement architecture that addresses their major pain points. She co-authored the authoritative guide on [[dbt-git-branching-strategies]] with [[Christine Berger]], [[Taylor Dunlap]], and [[Steve Dowling]].
