# Power BI

Power BI is Microsoft's business intelligence platform, growing from 28% to 33% adoption between 2023 and 2024. Its growth is driven by Microsoft 365 enterprise bundling.

## Market Position

- **Adoption:** 33% (growing)
- **Trend:** Growing, driven by Microsoft enterprise deals
- **Strengths:** "Free" with existing Microsoft licenses, enterprise integration, ease of use
- **Weaknesses:** Less capable for complex visualizations than Tableau, Microsoft ecosystem lock-in

## Competitive Landscape

Power BI is the primary challenger to [[Tableau]]'s enterprise BI leadership. It competes with [[Looker]] (22%, stable in tech companies) and open-source tools like Metabase and Superset (combined 20%, growing).
