# Hannes Mühleisen

Hannes Mühleisen is one of the creators of [[DuckDB]] and the founder of [[DuckDB Labs]], the commercial company providing support and extensions for DuckDB. He is also co-author, along with [[Mark Raasveldt]], of the DuckLake manifesto, which critiques the file‑based metadata architecture of [[Iceberg]] and [[Delta Lake]] and proposes [[DuckLake]] as an alternative that uses a SQL database for all lakehouse metadata. He is frequently interviewed and quoted in discussions about DuckDB's architecture, use cases, and future direction.
