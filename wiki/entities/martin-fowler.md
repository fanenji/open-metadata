# Martin Fowler

Martin Fowler is a thought leader and author of "Data Mesh Principles and Logical Architecture," a foundational article defining the four principles of [[data-mesh]]: domain ownership, data as a product, self-serve data platform, and federated computational governance. His work is referenced in the [[data-mesh-maturity-assessment]] as a primary source for the principles that underpin the assessment framework.
