# Goose

Goose is an extensible, open-source AI agent used in workshops and tutorials to demonstrate the power of the [[model-context-procotol]] when paired with a metadata platform. It serves as a practical example of an agent that can leverage a [[knowledge-graph]] to perform complex, context-aware tasks.
