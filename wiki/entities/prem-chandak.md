# Prem Chandak

Author of the case study "I Replaced a Production Data Pipeline with AI Agents — Here’s What Actually Happened." Chandak is a data engineer who designed and deployed an [[agent-based-etl-pipeline]] using the [[router-transform-validator-architecture]] with Claude Sonnet 4. His experiment replaced a legacy Airflow-based ETL pipeline with autonomous AI agents, achieving significant improvements in incident reduction and processing success rate. He advocates for [[llm-agent-guardrails]] including explicit boundaries, human-in-the-loop approval, and observability dashboards.
