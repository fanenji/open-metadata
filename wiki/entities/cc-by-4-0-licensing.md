# CC BY 4.0 Licensing

Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) is the preferred open license for High-Value Datasets under EU Implementing Regulation 2023/138. It requires attribution to the data provider but otherwise allows unrestricted reuse. CC0 (Public Domain Dedication) is also permitted as an equivalent or less restrictive alternative. Existing datasets published under older licenses (e.g., CC-BY 3.0) must be upgraded to CC BY 4.0 by June 9, 2024.
