# Q. Wu

Q. Wu is the author and creator of the [[GeoAI]] Python package. Wu published the JOSS paper "GeoAI: A Python package for integrating artificial intelligence with geospatial data analysis and visualization" (2026) and authored the book *GeoAI with Python: A Practical Guide to Open-Source Geospatial AI* (2026). Wu is associated with the [[opengeos]] organization.
