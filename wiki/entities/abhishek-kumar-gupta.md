# Abhishek Kumar Gupta

Expert in AI, Cloud, and Data Engineering, practitioner and author who focuses on building production-grade data pipelines, scalable AI systems, and bringing software engineering best practices to data pipelines. He writes on Medium under the publication *Tech with Abhishek*, covering real-world AI, cloud, and data engineering topics with a focus on dbt, the modern data stack, and AI-driven data engineering.

He is the author of:

- *dbt and Data Contracts: Enabling Reliable, API-Driven Analytics* (2025), which covers operationalizing data contracts with dbt, including YAML specification, preflight validation, model versioning, CI/CD integration, and artifact-based metadata surfacing.
- *Mastering dbt Deployment: Multi-Environment, CI/CD, and Promotion Strategies*, with emphasis on CI/CD, deployment strategies, and scalable dbt architectures.
- *[[abhishek-2025-running-dbt-real-world|Running dbt in the Real World: Cost Control, Governance, and Team Practices at Scale]]*, published on Medium, which provides experience-based guidance on operating dbt at scale, covering cost-aware engineering, governance role definitions, minimum model standards, team workflows, and operational maturity practices including cost dashboards and runbooks.
- *[[the-dbt-mcp-server-how-ai-finally-gets-to-talk-to-your-data-the-right-way|The dbt MCP Server: How AI Finally Gets to Talk to Your Data (the Right Way)]]* (2025), published on Medium, which provides comprehensive documentation of the [[dbt-mcp-server]]'s capabilities, setup procedures, and architectural significance, positioning it as the missing link between governed data engineering and AI systems.
