# Netflix

The origin company of Apache Iceberg. Iceberg was initially developed at Netflix to solve data lake scalability and correctness challenges. The article cautions against "do what Netflix does" reasoning, arguing that Iceberg's broader adoption is driven by common enterprise pain points rather than Netflix's specific use case.
