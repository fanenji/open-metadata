# Jonathan Talmi

Engineer at Snap who coined the term **dbt observability** in a post on the dbt Discourse forum. His framing of monitoring dbt run and test results as a distinct observability practice was cited by [[Or Avidov]] as the inspiration for the implementation guide.

## Contributions

- Named and defined the concept of dbt observability.
- Identified the need for infrastructure to monitor dbt execution at scale, especially in large projects with parallel invocations.
