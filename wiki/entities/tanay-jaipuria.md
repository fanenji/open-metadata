# Tanay Jaipuria

Author of the foundational article "[[understanding-the-modern-data-stack|Understanding the Modern Data Stack]]" (July 2022), which provides a 101/102-level overview of the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]] as a suite of cloud-native products for data integration and analysis. The article breaks down the stack into components including data sources, integration, storage/querying, transformation, governance/monitoring, and data uses.
