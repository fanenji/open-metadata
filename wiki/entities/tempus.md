# Tempus

Tempus is a healthcare AI company where Noah Kennedy's team adopted the [[elementary-dbt-package]] for dbt observability. The team's experience with Elementary — including Slack alerts, the reporting dashboard, and anomaly detection — is documented in the article "[[Elementary on dbt: An Overview]]".

Before adopting Elementary, the Tempus team relied on a custom regex/entrypoint solution for Slack alerts and a Data Studio dashboard built on a log sink for test monitoring.
