# OpenMetadata Business Glossary

The OpenMetadata Business Glossary is a hierarchical taxonomy of business terms with definitions, forming the governance foundation for consistent data understanding across an organization. It is part of the Governance domain within the [[openmetadata-standards-overview]] specification.

The glossary enables organizations to define and manage business terms, their relationships, and their mappings to technical data assets. It supports hierarchical tag structures, sensitive data classification (PII/PHI, GDPR), and governance policies. This provides a structured way to bridge the gap between business semantics and technical metadata.

The glossary model is expressed in all three representation formats (JSON Schema, RDF/OWL, JSON-LD), enabling both programmatic access and semantic reasoning. It connects to the wiki's existing coverage of [[automated-data-discovery-and-classification]] and [[data-domain-governance]] by providing the formal taxonomy structure that underpins those processes.
