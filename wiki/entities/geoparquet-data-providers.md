# GeoParquet Data Providers

A catalog of organizations providing datasets in GeoParquet format as of October 2023.

## Major Providers

- **Microsoft**: GeoParquet versions of all STAC catalogs; partitioned building footprints dataset
- **Planet**: RapidAI4EO STAC dataset converted to GeoParquet
- **Maxar**: Open Data STAC converted to GeoParquet
- **Ordnance Survey**: UK National Geographic Database Boundaries Collection
- **VIDA**: Combined Google + Microsoft building footprints (2.5B+ rows, ~200GB)
- **Streambatch**: ESA World Cereals dataset
- **Pacific Spatial Solutions**: Tokyo building and risk data
- **CARTO**: Data Observatory datasets (demographics, environmental, POI, mobility, financial)

## Community Conversions

- Chris Holmes: Google Building Footprints, Overture Maps (converted from Parquet), EuroCrops, NYC Taxi Zones

## Hosting

Most datasets are hosted on [[Source Cooperative]], Radiant Earth's open data platform.

## Relevance to Project

These providers demonstrate the growing availability of geospatial data in GeoParquet format, supporting the project's evaluation of GeoParquet as a standard data exchange format.
