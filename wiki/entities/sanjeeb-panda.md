# Sanjeeb Panda

Data and AI Engineer at Amazon. Author of the forward-looking article "The 2026 Data Engineering Roadmap: Building Data Systems for the Agentic AI Era," which argues that data engineering is shifting from moving data to architecting context for AI agents.

Panda's work emphasizes the rise of [[context-engineering]], [[active-metadata-management]], [[vector-database-operations]], [[agent-friendly-api-design]], [[ai-data-governance]], and [[data-quality-for-ai]] as the core competencies for data engineers in the agentic AI era.
