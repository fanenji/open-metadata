# Antoine Lefebvre

Antoine Lefebvre is a Software Architecture enthusiast at [[BlaBlaCar]], with particular interest in APIs Design and Data modelling. He is the author of the articles "Data Pipelines Architecture at BlaBlaCar" and "Scaling Success: The dbt Ecosystem at BlaBlaCar", which describe BlaBlaCar's internal data infrastructure and dbt ecosystem. The former documents BlaBlaCar's evolution toward a modern [[elt-pattern]] organized as a [[data-mesh]], with detailed coverage of ingestion strategies, dbt integration via a custom [[dbt-dag-generator]], reverse ETL patterns, and feature store architecture. The latter explores topics such as development environment isolation and containerization for dbt, reflecting the organization's practices as the dbt ecosystem scaled.
