# Radiant Earth

An organization that promotes open geospatial standards, particularly the [[stac-spatiotemporal-asset-catalog]] (STAC) specification. Radiant Earth's GitHub page is recommended by [[scott-parks]] as an excellent starting point for learning about STAC. The organization also hosted the interview with Scott about his cloud-native geospatial journey.
