# Codastra

Codastra is the author/organization behind the article "DuckDB Geospatial: Vector Tiles, Rasters, and Fast Maps on Parquet." The article explores using DuckDB's spatial extension to build production-ready web maps directly from Parquet and GeoParquet files without a heavyweight GIS stack.
