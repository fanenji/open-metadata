# ArchitectKB

**ArchitectKB** is a complementary "knowledge vault" designed to capture and persist architectural knowledge, patterns, and institutional insights.

While tools like [[arc-kit]] focus on the generation of project-specific governance artifacts (which have a lifecycle of creation, review, and eventual supersession), [[architectkb]] serves as the long-term repository for persistent knowledge that transcends individual projects.

The synergy between the two lies in the workflow: [[arc-kit]] generates the structured artifacts, and the key insights from those artifacts are distilled into [[architectkb]] to facilitate [[knowledge-compulating-in-architecture]].
