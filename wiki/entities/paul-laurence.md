# Paul Laurence

Paul Laurence is an author at The New Stack and a representative of [[Crunchy Data]]. He wrote the article "Unleashing Postgres for Analytics With DuckDB Integration," which describes the integration of [[DuckDB]] into PostgreSQL via the Crunchy Bridge managed service.
