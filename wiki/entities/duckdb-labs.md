# DuckDB Labs

DuckDB Labs is a commercial company formed by [[Hannes Mühleisen]] and the other creators of [[DuckDB]] in July 2021. Based in Amsterdam, the company has approximately 18 employees. DuckDB Labs provides core development and support for DuckDB, which is free and open source under the MIT license with no dual license or open core model. The company monetizes the project by offering commercial support, custom extensions, and custom versions of DuckDB.

## Activities

- Core development of [[DuckDB]] and its extension ecosystem, including custom extensions for clients.
- Development and support of the [[DuckDB Spatial]] extension.
- Providing commercial support and consulting for DuckDB deployments.

## Relationship with MotherDuck

DuckDB Labs announced a strategic partnership with [[MotherDuck]] in November 2022, with DuckDB Labs providing core DuckDB expertise and MotherDuck building the managed cloud layer.
