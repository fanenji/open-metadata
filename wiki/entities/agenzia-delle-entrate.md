# Agenzia delle Entrate

The Italian Revenue Agency. It is the data owner for addresses (co-owner with ISTAT for ANNCSU) and cadastral parcels. The agency is conducting legal and technical analyses to determine how ANNCSU data can be published in compliance with the HVD regulation and GDPR requirements.
