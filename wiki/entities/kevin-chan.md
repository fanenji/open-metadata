# Kevin Chan

Kevin Chan is a data engineer at [[Snapcommerce]] who helped scale the data platform from infancy. He is one of the original developers of the dbt Snowflake run operation. Alongside [[Jonathan Talmi]], he co-presented "Observability within dbt" at Coalesce 2021, demonstrating a lightweight artifact-based monitoring and alerting system built with dbt, Airflow, Snowflake, Looker, and Slack.

Chan's background includes experience in engineering, machine learning engineering, and deployments. He is a graduate of the University of Waterloo in computer science and statistics, and previously worked at Hootsuite and Splunk.
