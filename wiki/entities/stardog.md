# Stardog

An RDF/OWL graph database with inference engine capabilities. Mentioned in [[Alexander Shereshevsky]]'s article as an example of the formal ontology approach to knowledge graphs. Used in enterprise settings requiring regulatory explainability and complex hierarchical reasoning.
