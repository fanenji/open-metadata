# Trend Micro

Trend Micro is a cybersecurity company that conducted research on exposed [[model-context-protocol]] (MCP) servers. They identified 492 confirmed instances running without client authentication or traffic encryption, noting that the convenience of MCP led organizations into a "false sense of security."

## Key Findings
- 492 confirmed instances of unauthenticated MCP servers
- Identified MCP servers as gateways to the broader cloud environment
- Published "Update on Exposed MCP Servers: The Threat Widens to the Cloud"
