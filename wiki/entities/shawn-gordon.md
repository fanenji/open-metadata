# Shawn Gordon

Author of the article "Ground Your AI in Standards-Based Knowledge with Ontology Explorer," published on the OpenMetadata blog (April 30, 2026). The article introduces Ontology Explorer, a feature shipping in OpenMetadata 1.13 (Collate 1.13), and argues for ontology-based AI grounding over similarity-based context engineering.
