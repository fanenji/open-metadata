# Assaf Lavi

Assaf Lavi is a Principal Engineer at [[nexar]]. He specializes in building large-scale geospatial analytics layers, transforming raw GPS and vision data into searchable, semantically enriched digital twins of road networks. In 2022, he presented "Complex Geospatial Analytics with dbt" at the Big Things Conference, describing Nexar's approach to scalable geospatial data engineering using dbt, Snowflake, H3, OpenStreetMap, and Who's on First.
