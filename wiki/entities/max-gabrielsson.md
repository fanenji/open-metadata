# Max Gabrielsson

Max Gabrielsson is a software engineer at [[DuckDB Labs]], where he works on DuckDB's execution layer and the [[DuckDB Spatial]] extension. He holds a bachelor's degree in computer science from Uppsala University. Gabrielsson is the primary developer of the DuckDB Spatial extension, which adds geospatial capabilities to DuckDB via a Simple Features vector geometry type modeled after PostGIS.

## Contributions

- Lead developer of the [[DuckDB Spatial]] extension
- Contributor to DuckDB's vectorized execution layer
- Speaker at GeoPython 2024 on "DuckDB Spatial: Supercharged Geospatial SQL"
