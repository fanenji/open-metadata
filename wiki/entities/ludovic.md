# Ludovic

Ludovic is the founder and creator of [[Kestra]], the open-source declarative YAML-based orchestrator. He has publicly stated that Kestra is not intended to replace [[Apache Airflow]] for all users, but rather targets teams hitting Airflow pain points. Happy Airflow users need not migrate.
