# Florent Gravin

Florent Gravin presented the talk "Towards Better Data Platforms with Semantic Metadata" at FOSS4G Europe 2024 on behalf of Olivia Guyot. He works at [[camp-to-camp]] and is involved in the development and advocacy of semantic metadata approaches for geospatial data platforms, particularly around [[geonetwork]] and [[dcat-ontology]].
