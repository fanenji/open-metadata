# INSPIRE Directive (2007/2/CE)

The INSPIRE Directive establishes an Infrastructure for Spatial Information in the European Community. It is the foundational framework for geospatial data interoperability in the EU. For HVD geospatial datasets, INSPIRE compliance is mandatory, including the implementation of network services (WMS, WFS) and adherence to INSPIRE data models and metadata standards. The directive was transposed into Italian law via D.Lgs 32/2010.
