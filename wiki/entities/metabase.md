# Metabase

An open-source business intelligence (BI) tool used for data exploration, visualization, and ad-hoc analysis. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], Metabase is one of the popular BI tools alongside [[looker]], [[tableau]], and [[mode]].
