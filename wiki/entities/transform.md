# Transform

A company in the emerging metrics layer space of the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]]. Transform helps organizations define commonly used business metrics (e.g., monthly active users, ARR, churn) in a standardized way in code, ensuring consistent definitions across the organization.
