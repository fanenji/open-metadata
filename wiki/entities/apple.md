# Apple

Enterprise adopter of Apache Iceberg. Apple has publicly discussed using Iceberg as part of its lakehouse, with an engineering manager describing Iceberg use "all over the place," spanning hundreds of MB to many PB, with investments in maintenance procedures and row-level operations for regulatory compliance.
