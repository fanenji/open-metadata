# LookML

[[looker|Looker's]] modeling language used for data transformation. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], LookML is an alternative to [[dbt]] for defining transformations directly within the Looker BI platform, alongside other options like [[apache-airflow]] and [[delta-live-tables]].
