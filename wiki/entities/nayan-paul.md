# Nayan Paul

**Nayan Paul** is the author of the article "The Semantic Layer Generator: When Agentic AI Meets Data Architecture" (published April 2026). He proposes the [[semantic-layer-generator]] concept and advocates for [[forward-deployed-engineering-data]] as a design philosophy for agentic AI systems in data architecture.

Paul's work focuses on automating the semantic layer — the translation between normalized database schemas and flat, query-ready wide tables — using a hybrid deterministic-probabilistic pipeline (see [[agentic-data-pipeline-pattern]]). He argues that LLMs, agentic orchestration, and MCP have matured enough to make this automation reliable in production.

He offers 1-1 consulting sessions on agentic AI topics via Calendly.
