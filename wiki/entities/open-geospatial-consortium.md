# Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC)

The Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) is an international standards organization that promotes the development and adoption of open standards for geospatial data and services. The OGC promotes GeoParquet as an open, cloud-native standard for storing geospatial vector data in the Apache Parquet columnar format.
