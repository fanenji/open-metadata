# Alex Merced

Alex Merced is the Head of Developer Relations at [[Dremio]] and a Developer Advocate. He is the author of the hands-on guide *Dremio Lakehouse in Action with Iceberg & dbt* and co-author of *Apache Iceberg: The Definitive Guide* (O'Reilly). He is the maintainer of the [[dremioframe]] library and creator of the [[dremio-simple-query]] library, which simplifies querying Dremio via [[Apache Arrow Flight]] and converting results to DuckDB relations. Merced creates tutorials, demos, and educational content focused on the Dremio Lakehouse, Apache Iceberg, Project Nessie, dbt integration, and Dremio's integration with DuckDB. He is a regular contributor to Dremio's Medium publication "Data, Analytics & AI with Dremio". He authored and presented a BrightTALK webinar titled "Unlock the Potential of Data Analytics with Dremio and DuckDB", which discusses the complementary roles of Dremio and DuckDB in modern data analytics workflows. He also authored the article "Using DuckDB with Your Dremio Data Lakehouse", describing a cost-optimization architecture combining Dremio and DuckDB. His work emphasizes open data lakehouse architectures that avoid vendor lock-in, leveraging open formats like [[Apache Iceberg]] and [[Apache Arrow]].
