# EU Commission Implementing Regulation 2023/138

The EU Implementing Regulation adopted on December 21, 2022, that establishes a list of specific High-Value Datasets (HVD) and their publication and reuse modalities. It defines six thematic categories, specific datasets within each category, required attributes, formats, licenses (CC BY 4.0 or CC0), and access methods (API + bulk download). The regulation applies from June 9, 2024, and also applies retroactively to existing datasets.
