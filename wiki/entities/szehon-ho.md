# Szehon Ho

Szehon Ho is an Apache Iceberg PMC Member and Software Engineer at [[Databricks]]. He previously worked at Apple on Apache Iceberg.

He has been a speaker on several topics:

- **Geospatial Tables in the Open Lakehouse: A New Era for Iceberg & Parquet** — Detailed the technical debates and design decisions behind the Iceberg GEO v3 specification, co-authored with [[Jia Yu]]. He also provided a cautious comparison of Iceberg vs. Delta Lake for geospatial workloads.
- **Iceberg's Best Secret: Exploring Metadata Tables** — Covered Iceberg's hidden system tables (snapshots, manifests, files, history) and their use for governance, debugging, and data discovery.
