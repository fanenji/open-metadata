# AgID (Agenzia per l'Italia Digitale)

AgID is the Italian government agency responsible for the digital transformation of the public administration. It coordinates the implementation of the EU Open Data Directive and the High-Value Datasets (HVD) regulation in Italy. AgID published the operational guide for HVD datasets and maintains key national registries including the RNDT (Repertorio Nazionale Dati Territoriali) and the DCAT-AP_IT metadata standard.
