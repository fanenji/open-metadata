# Datacoves

**Datacoves** is a managed DataOps platform for [dbt](https://www.getdbt.com/) and [Apache Airflow](https://airflow.apache.org/). It is the maintainer and sponsor of the open-source [[dbt-checkpoint]] project.

## Platform Features

- **Private cloud or SaaS** deployment options
- **Managed dbt + Airflow** — production-ready from day one
- **In-browser VS Code** — rapid developer onboarding
- **Bring your own tools** — integrates with existing stack, no lock-in
- **AI-assisted development** — connects to approved LLMs (Anthropic, OpenAI, Azure, Gemini, etc.)
- **Built-in governance** — CI/CD, guardrails, and best practices included

## Relationship to dbt-checkpoint

Datacoves maintains [[dbt-checkpoint]] as an open-source tool that catches mistakes at commit time. The Datacoves platform extends this philosophy by preventing quality issues from occurring in the first place through built-in governance and best practices.
