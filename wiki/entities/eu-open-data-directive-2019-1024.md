# EU Open Data Directive (2019/1024)

The EU Directive on open data and the re-use of public sector information (the "Open Data Directive"). It introduced the concept of High-Value Datasets (HVD) and identified six thematic categories. The directive was transposed into Italian law via Legislative Decree 36/2006. It mandates that public sector data should be open by default and machine-readable.
