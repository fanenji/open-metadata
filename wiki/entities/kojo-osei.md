# Kojo Osei

Kojo Osei is an investor at Matrix Partners. His blog post about [[DuckDB]] (June 2022) introduced the framework categorizing databases along two axes — OLAP vs OLTP and standalone vs embedded — to illustrate the innovation gap that DuckDB fills. This framework is heavily cited in the article "[[DuckDB — What’s the Hype About?]]".
