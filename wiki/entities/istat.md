# ISTAT (Istituto Nazionale di Statistica)

ISTAT is the Italian National Institute of Statistics. It is the data owner for several High-Value Datasets, including administrative units (boundaries), statistical data (industrial production, CPI, national accounts, population, poverty, etc.), and addresses (co-owner with Agenzia delle Entrate for ANNCSU). ISTAT data is currently published under CC-BY 3.0 license and must be upgraded to CC-BY 4.0 by June 2024.
