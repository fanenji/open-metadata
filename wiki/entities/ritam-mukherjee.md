# Ritam Mukherjee

Author of the article "Integrating LLMs and AI Agents into Data Engineering Workflows" published on the Data Engineer Things Medium publication. Mukherjee advocates for an augmentative (copilot) approach to AI in data engineering, where LLMs and AI agents automate repetitive tasks such as boilerplate SQL, lineage tracing, and constraint checking, ultimately automating 30-40% of repetitive work. The article provides practical code examples using OpenAI's GPT-4o and LangChain for generating dbt data quality tests and analyzing Spark logs. Mukherjee emphasizes human-in-the-loop review, fine-tuned or self-hosted models, and unit tests as mitigations for AI hallucinations and reliability concerns.
