# Reliable Data Engineering

Reliable Data Engineering is the author and organization behind multiple articles focused on practical data‑engineering solutions, particularly around dbt project scaling and standardization. Their philosophy emphasizes "boring but correct" patterns over clever, sophisticated solutions. The team consists of 8 analytics engineers and 12 analysts managing 340 dbt models, 89 macros, and 47M+ rows of data, providing detailed case studies of advanced dbt anti‑patterns and their remediation. Their documented results include a 65% reduction in dbt run time, 85% reduction in data quality incidents, and 63% reduction in Snowflake compute costs. Their website is [reliable-data-engineering.netlify.app](https://reliable-data-engineering.netlify.app/).

## Known Works

- **Standardizing Logging in dbt: How We Made Debugging Easier for Everyone** (2025) — A guide to overriding dbt’s built-in `log` macro for consistent, context‑aware logging across dbt projects.
- **The Modern Data Stack in 2025: What Actually Won** — A data‑driven analysis of the modern data ecosystem, drawing from multiple industry surveys, job market data, vendor signals, and practitioner surveys.
- **Top 5 Advanced dbt Anti-Patterns That Nearly Killed Our Analytics Team** — A case study of advanced dbt anti‑patterns and their remediation, including the team’s composition, specific anti‑patterns encountered, and measured results.
