# Vikram Gundeti

CTO at [[Foursquare]]. Vikram Gundeti was a speaker on the livestream "Geospatial Tables in the Open Lakehouse: A New Era for Iceberg & Parquet," providing an industry adoption perspective. He described Foursquare's migration from custom folder structures and JSON formats to Iceberg tables, highlighting benefits in flexible querying, schema evolution, and GDPR compliance. He also emphasized the importance of storage-compute separation for data providers.
