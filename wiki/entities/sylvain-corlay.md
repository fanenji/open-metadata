# Sylvain Corlay

Sylvain Corlay is a core developer of Project Jupyter, co-author of the xtensor library, and an entrepreneur at [[QuantStack]]. He is the lead author of the [[JupyterGIS]] announcement and a key contributor to collaborative editing infrastructure in JupyterLab.

He is also involved in the [[JupyterCAD]] project, which shares architectural patterns with JupyterGIS.
