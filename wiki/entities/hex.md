# Hex

An analytics and collaboration tool that makes it easier for data analysts and scientists to query, visualize, and collaborate on data. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], Hex is used alongside [[noteable]] for analytics and collaboration workflows.
