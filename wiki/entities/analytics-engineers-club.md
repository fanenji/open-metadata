# Analytics Engineers Club

The Analytics Engineers Club is a training program that offers a 10-week course titled "From Analysts to Engineer." The program is taught by a former [[dbt Labs]] employee and aims to help data analysts transition into analytics engineering roles using [[dbt Core]].
