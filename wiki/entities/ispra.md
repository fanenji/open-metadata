# ISPRA (Istituto Superiore per la Protezione e la Ricerca Ambientale)

ISPRA is the Italian Institute for Environmental Protection and Research. It is the data owner for numerous environmental HVD datasets including hydrography, protected sites, geology, soil, species distribution, air quality, climate, emissions, waste, water, and noise data. ISPRA coordinates with regional environmental agencies (ARPA/APPA) for data collection and publication.
