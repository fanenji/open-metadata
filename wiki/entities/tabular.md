# Tabular

Company founded by Ryan Blue, Daniel Weeks, and Jason Reid, the original creators of Apache Iceberg. Acquired by Databricks in June 2024, which put more commercial muscle behind the core Iceberg team and made Iceberg interoperability a top-tier strategic priority for a major platform vendor.
