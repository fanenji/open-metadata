# Georg Heiler

Author of the article "Geospatial binning with hexagons on spark," which describes techniques for spatial bucketing using H3 hexagons on Apache Spark. The article is referenced in the [[hex-egg-table]] synthesis as supporting literature for the H3-based spatial aggregation pattern.
