# decube

decube is a data trust platform for the GenAI era, founded by [[Jatin Solanki]]. The platform offers a data contracts module and is positioned as a solution for implementing [[data-contract]]s at scale. It is listed alongside [[datakitchen]], [[dremio]], and the open-source [[datacontract.com]] as a data contract platform option.
