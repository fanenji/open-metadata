# IGM (Istituto Geografico Militare)

IGM is the Italian Military Geographic Institute. It is the data owner for geographic names and state borders (administrative units). IGM maintains the Confine di Stato (state border) in accordance with international agreements. Currently, IGM data lacks metadata, APIs, and proper open licenses. The institute is working to implement INSPIRE-compliant network services and GeoPackage formats.
