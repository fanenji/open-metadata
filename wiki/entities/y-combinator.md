# Y Combinator

Y Combinator (YC) is a startup accelerator that provided early-stage funding and mentorship to Rapportive, Martin Kleppmann's second startup. Rapportive was part of an early YC batch, when the accelerator was still relatively young but had already built a strong reputation.

## Relevance

YC's program required the founders to relocate from the UK to San Francisco for three months, which led to Kleppmann establishing a presence in the Bay Area and eventually selling to LinkedIn.
