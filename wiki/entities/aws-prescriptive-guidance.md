# AWS Prescriptive Guidance

AWS Prescriptive Guidance published a "Data Mesh Strategy Framework" that provides a five-phase strategic roadmap for [[data-mesh]] adoption:

1. **Discover** — Assess current state and identify opportunities
2. **Align** — Define domain boundaries and governance model
3. **Launch** — Pilot with 2-3 domains
4. **Scale** — Onboard additional domains systematically
5. **Evolve** — Optimize and expand the ecosystem

The framework emphasizes alignment with business value over technology tools and is one of several frameworks synthesized in the [[data-mesh-maturity-assessment]].
