# Greenplum

Greenplum is a data source connector for [[OpenMetadata]] that was contributed by the community and included in the 1.2.0 release. It is part of the growing library of [[openmetadata-connectors|OpenMetadata connectors]] that enable metadata ingestion from various data platforms.

The connector was highlighted during the October 2023 community meeting as an example of community contributions being integrated into the platform.
