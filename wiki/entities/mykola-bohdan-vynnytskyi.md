# Mykola-Bohdan Vynnytskyi

Data engineer, consultant, YouTuber, and course creator. Author of the "Understanding dbt" series on Medium, which covers dbt fundamentals including models, tests, and monitoring. Creator of the Parquet Reader app and a Udemy practice test course for the dbt Analytics Engineering certification.

## Contributions

- **"Understanding dbt: Monitoring"** — A beginner-friendly tutorial on monitoring dbt projects, covering both dbt Cloud and dbt Core approaches.
- **"Understanding dbt: Tests"** — A companion article on dbt testing practices.
- **Udemy course**: dbt Analytics Engineering Certification practice exam with over 300 questions.

## Relevance

Vynnytskyi's work provides accessible, practical guidance for teams adopting dbt, particularly those using dbt Core who need DIY monitoring solutions. His articles serve as entry points to concepts more deeply covered in other wiki sources.
