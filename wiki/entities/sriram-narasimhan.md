# Sriram Narasimhan

**Sriram Narasimhan** is VP Engineering, Data & Analytics at SAP Procurement. He is the author of the multi-part series "The Semantic Layer: The Trust Fabric of the Agentic Enterprise," which argues for the critical role of semantic layers in enabling trustworthy, explainable agentic AI systems.

## Key Contributions

- Defined the [[business-semantics-evolution]] framework (Dashboards → Metrics Layers → Semantic Layers → Agentic Layers).
- Articulated the [[enterprise-ai-trust-gap]] as the #1 barrier to enterprise AI adoption.
- Positioned the [[semantic-layer]] as the essential foundation for the [[agentic-enterprise]].
- Introduced the [[synthai-synthesis-ai]] concept (citing a16z) as the next wave of enterprise AI requiring semantic understanding.
