# Airbyte

**Airbyte** is an open-source [[elt-pattern|ELT]] data integration tool that provides connectors to extract and load data from various sources into cloud data warehouses. It is one of the most popular tools in the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]] for the data integration layer, competing with [[Fivetran]] and [[Portable]].

Airbyte has surprised the market with rapid adoption, growing from 11% to 21% market share (+91% YoY) between 2023 and 2024. This growth has been driven by several factors:

## Growth Drivers

- **Open-source model** appeals to cost-conscious teams
- **300+ connectors** (community-driven) provide broad coverage
- **Self-hosted option** eliminates per-row pricing, avoiding the unpredictable costs associated with competitors' MAR models
- **Strong documentation and community** support

## Market Position

Airbyte is the primary challenger to [[Fivetran]]'s market leadership. Its growth is driven by teams seeking to avoid Fivetran's MAR model and the consolidating nature of the data ingestion space, where a few major players are emerging. Airbyte is positioned as the fast follower, offering an open-source alternative that aligns with the modern data stack's preference for flexible, low-cost solutions.

Additionally, Airbyte competes with [[Portable]] in the open-source data integration space, reinforcing its role as a key player in the data ingestion layer of the modern data stack.
