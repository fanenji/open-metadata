# Christine Berger

Christine Berger is a Resident Architect at [[dbt Labs]]. Her day-to-day work involves helping teams reach their technical and business-driven goals by guiding them to implement architecture that addresses their major pain points. She co-authored the authoritative guide on [[dbt-git-branching-strategies]] with [[Carol Ohms]], [[Taylor Dunlap]], and [[Steve Dowling]].
