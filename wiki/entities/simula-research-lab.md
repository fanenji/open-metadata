# Simula Research Lab

Simula Research Lab (Simula) is a Norwegian research organization and a consortium partner in the [[JupyterGIS]] project funded by the European Space Agency. Simula has a long history of contributions to the Jupyter project and the broader open-source scientific computing ecosystem.

Co-authors [[Anne Fouilloux]] and [[Monika Weissschnur]] are affiliated with Simula.
