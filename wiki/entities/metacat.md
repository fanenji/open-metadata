# Metacat

An open-source data catalog solution originally developed by Netflix. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], Metacat is one of several open-source options for data cataloging, documentation, and discovery, alongside [[amundsen]] (Lyft) and [[datahub]] (LinkedIn), competing with commercial solutions like [[atlan]].
