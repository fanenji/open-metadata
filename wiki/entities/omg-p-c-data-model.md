# OMG P&C Data Model

The **OMG Property and Casualty (P&C) Data Model** is an enterprise-grade relational database schema used as the primary dataset in the benchmark study.

Created by the **Object Management Group (OMG)**, it represents a complex insurance domain with 199 tables and 2/43 foreign keys in its full version. The benchmark specifically utilizes a subset of 13 tables to test the limits of LLM-based Text-to-SQL and Text-to-SPARQL capabilities.
