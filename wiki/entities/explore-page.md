# Explore Page

The Explore Page is a UI feature in OpenMetadata that allows users to discover, view, and filter data assets. It is the primary interface for navigating the metadata catalog.

## Data Product Integration

When [[data-products|Data Products]] are created, their constituent assets can be viewed and filtered on the Explore Page. This provides a way to locate all assets belonging to a specific Data Product, enhancing discoverability.

## Relationship to Hierarchy View

The Explore Page may also support the [[hierarchy-view|Hierarchy View]], which organizes assets in a tree-like structure based on relationships and dependencies. The exact relationship between the Explore Page and the Hierarchy View is not fully documented.
