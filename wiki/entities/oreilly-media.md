# O'Reilly Media

O'Reilly Media is the publisher of *Designing Data-Intensive Applications*. They were described by Martin Kleppmann as "laid-back" with deadlines for the first edition, allowing him to take the time needed to make the book high quality. For the second edition, they were more aggressive about deadlines due to high anticipation.
