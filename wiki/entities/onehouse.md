# Onehouse

Onehouse is a technology company that published guidance on securing data lakehouses, covering data encryption, access control, and compliance best practices. Their guidance is relevant to [[model-context-protocol]] (MCP) deployments in [[data-lakehouse]] environments.

## Key Findings
- Published "Securing Your Data Lakehouse: Best Practices for Data Encryption, Access Control, and Compliance"
- Covers AES-256 encryption, access controls, and regulatory compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA)
- Their guidance informs [[mcp-security-best-practices]] for lakehouse environments
