# BigQuery

BigQuery is Google Cloud's serverless data warehouse, holding a stable ~24% adoption rate. It is the GCP-native choice for data warehousing.

## Market Position

- **Adoption:** 24% (stable)
- **Trend:** Gains coming primarily from GCP migrations
- **Strengths:** Serverless model, tight GCP integration, cost-conscious appeal
- **Weaknesses:** Less ecosystem breadth than Snowflake or Databricks

## Platformization

BigQuery is expanding its platform capabilities:
- BigQuery ML
- Dataform (transformation, acquired by Google)
- Connected Sheets (spreadsheet integration)
- BI Engine

This [[platformization]] trend is consistent across all major warehouse vendors.
