# Jia Yu

Co-Founder and CTO at [[Wherobots]]. Jia Yu moderated the livestream "Geospatial Tables in the Open Lakehouse: A New Era for Iceberg & Parquet" and is a key contributor to the Apache Iceberg GEO v3 specification. He led the implementation of Iceberg GEO types in Wherobots' Havasu engine, providing an early reference implementation for the community.
