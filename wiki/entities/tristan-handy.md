# Tristan Handy

Founder and CEO of dbt Labs (formerly Fishtown Analytics). Author of the influential article "The Modern Data Stack: Past, Present, and Future" which traces the evolution of the data tooling ecosystem from 2012 through 2025.

Handy has been a central figure in shaping the modern data stack narrative. His analysis uses Carlotta Perez's S-curve framework to explain periods of rapid innovation and maturation in data infrastructure. He is a frequent speaker at data conferences and a thought leader on data engineering, analytics engineering, and the future of the data stack.

## Key Contributions

- Founded dbt (data build tool), which became the standard transformation layer in the modern data stack
- Articulated the three-phase narrative of the modern data stack: Cambrian Explosion I (2012-2016), Deployment (2016-2020), and Cambrian Explosion II (2021-2025)
- Identified five key opportunity areas for the next wave of data innovation: governance, real-time, reverse ETL, democratized exploration, and vertical analytical experiences
