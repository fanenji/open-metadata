# OpenAI

OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research and deployment company. Its Large Language Models (LLMs), such as GPT-4, serve as the foundational intelligence for many modern AI-powered data engineering use cases, including automated SQL generation, data quality rule creation, and pipeline documentation.
