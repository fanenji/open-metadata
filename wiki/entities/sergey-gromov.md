# Sergey Gromov

Author of the article "Secrets of the Semantic Layer in Big Tech: How Uber, Netflix, and Airbnb Manage Metrics" published on Towards AI. The article synthesizes publicly available engineering materials from major technology companies to reconstruct the architecture of enterprise-grade semantic layers.

Gromov argues that Big Tech treats the semantic layer as a platform infrastructure component rather than a BI feature, managing the full lifecycle of metrics including definition, computation, quality, serving, governance, and AI grounding.
