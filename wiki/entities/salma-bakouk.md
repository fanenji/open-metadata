# Salma Bakouk

Co-founder and CEO of **Sifflet**, a Full Data Stack Observability platform. Co-author of the article "Data Quality Monitoring is dead. Say Hello to Full Data Stack Observability" which argues for data observability as an evolution beyond traditional data quality monitoring and testing.
