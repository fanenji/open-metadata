# ANNCSU (Archivio Nazionale dei Numeri Civici e delle Strade Urbane)

The National Archive of Street Numbers and Urban Streets. It is the official national reference for street directories and address data for all Italian municipalities. Managed jointly by ISTAT and Agenzia delle Entrate, the ANNCSU contains street directories and civic numbers for all Italian municipalities. Its publication as an HVD dataset requires resolution of GDPR privacy concerns.
