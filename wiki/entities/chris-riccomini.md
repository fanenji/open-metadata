# Chris Riccomini

Chris Riccomini is a software engineer and author, best known for co-authoring the second edition of *Designing Data-Intensive Applications* with [[martin-kleppmann]]. He is also the author of *The Missing ReadMe*, a practical guide for new and aspiring software engineers covering topics beyond coding.

## Career

- **LinkedIn** — Worked with former colleague [[martin-kleppmann]] on stream processing infrastructure (Kafka, Samza).
- **Author** — Wrote *The Missing ReadMe*, a guide for new and aspiring software engineers covering topics beyond coding.
- **Newsletter** — Writes *Materialized View*, a newsletter on latest trends in data systems.
- **Startup Investor** — Active as an investor in data systems startups.

## Role in DDIA 2nd Edition

Kleppmann brought Riccomini on as co-author for the second edition because Riccomini was up to date on cutting-edge industry technology (cloud-native systems, data lakes, etc.) while Kleppmann had gone deeper into theory in academia. Riccomini contributed knowledge of latest industry trends and practices, while Kleppmann focused on teaching methodology and precise explanations. Their collaboration updated the book to reflect cloud-native systems architecture and other modern developments.
