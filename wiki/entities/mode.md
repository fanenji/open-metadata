# Mode

A business intelligence (BI) tool used for data exploration, visualization, and ad-hoc analysis. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], Mode is one of the popular BI tools alongside [[looker]], [[tableau]], and [[metabase]].
