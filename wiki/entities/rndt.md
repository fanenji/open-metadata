# RNDT (Repertorio Nazionale Dati Territoriali)

The National Registry of Territorial Data. It is the Italian national metadata catalog for spatial data, established under the INSPIRE Directive implementation (D.Lgs 32/2010). All geospatial HVD datasets must be documented in the RNDT with compliant metadata. The RNDT is maintained by AgID and follows the LG-RNDT guidelines.
