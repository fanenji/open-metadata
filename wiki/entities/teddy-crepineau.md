# Teddy Crépineau

Teddy Crépineau is the author of the blog post "[[Simple, Easy, and Efficient Data Quality with OpenMetadata]]", published on the OpenMetadata blog. The article promotes OpenMetadata's metadata-first approach to data quality, arguing for its superiority over standalone tools like [[Great Expectations]] and [[Soda]].

Crépineau's writing emphasizes the democratization of data quality through no-code UI, centralized metadata context for faster debugging, and organizational-level health dashboards. The article is a promotional piece from OpenMetadata's own publication and should be treated as marketing content rather than objective analysis.
