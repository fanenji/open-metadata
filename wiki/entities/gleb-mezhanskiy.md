# Gleb Mezhanskiy

Gleb Mezhanskiy is the author of the article "What is CI, and why should you care about it," published on the Datafold blog. He has a background in data engineering and advocates for applying software engineering CI/CD practices to data workflows. His article introduces the [[dbt-hierarchy-of-needs]] framework and provides practical guidance on implementing CI for dbt projects.
