# Noah Kennedy

Noah Kennedy is a Data Engineer at Meta (formerly at Tempus, a healthcare AI company). He is an author and advocate for dbt and data testing infrastructure. He wrote the article "[[Elementary on dbt: An Overview]]" which describes his team's adoption of the [[elementary-dbt-package]] for enhanced observability and alerting.

Kennedy describes himself as a "dbt evangelist" and a "huge advocate of the importance of proper testing infrastructures in a data pipeline." His writing focuses on practical, experience-based recommendations for data engineering teams.
