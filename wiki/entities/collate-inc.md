# Collate, Inc.

Collate, Inc. is the company behind the [[OpenMetadata]] open-source project. It provides commercial support, drives the project's development roadmap, and offers a SaaS version of the platform called **Collate Cloud** (available at `https://cloud.getcollate.io/`).

## Relationship to OpenMetadata

Collate maintains an open-core model where the core metadata management platform is fully open-source, while certain advanced operational features are exclusive to the Collate Cloud SaaS offering. Features announced as SaaS-only in the 1.2.0 release include:

- **[[cost-analysis|Cost Analysis]]**: Used vs. unused asset tracking with size and storage cost estimation.
- **[[knowledge-center|Knowledge Center]]**: Full-page article creation and documentation system.

The company also drives community engagement through regular community meetings, webinars, and the OpenMetadata Slack community.
