# Naidu Rongali

Sr Data & AI Engineer at Amazon. Patent holder for techniques in high volume data processing. Author of the article "Defining Data Quality: The Foundation of Modern Data Architecture" on Towards Data Engineering (Medium), which proposes a seven-dimension framework for data quality and a quantifiable Data Quality Score methodology.
