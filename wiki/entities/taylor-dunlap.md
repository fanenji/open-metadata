# Taylor Dunlap

Taylor Dunlap is an expert at [[dbt Labs]] who serves as a critical bridge between teams and business outcomes. He contributes to guiding teams on [[dbt-git-branching-strategies]] and aligning them on a comprehensive technical vision through identification of problems and solutions. He co-authored the authoritative guide on branching strategies with [[Christine Berger]], [[Carol Ohms]], and [[Steve Dowling]].
