# Hussein Jundi

Hussein Jundi is the author of the article "Data Engineering: Architectures & Strategies for Handling Sensitive Data," published on the Medium Data Science Collective. The article presents a maturity-based framework for selecting de-identification techniques (masking, pseudonymization, encryption) aligned with an organization's data maturity level.

Jundi's work emphasizes a pragmatic, constraint-driven approach to data governance, arguing that the simplest compliant solution (e.g., data masking) is often the best choice for low-maturity organizations, rather than pursuing ideal but impractical architectures.
