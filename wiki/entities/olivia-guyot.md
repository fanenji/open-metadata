# Olivia Guyot

Olivia Guyot is the original author of the talk "Towards Better Data Platforms with Semantic Metadata" presented at FOSS4G Europe 2024. She works at [[camp-to-camp]] and is involved in the development and advocacy of semantic metadata approaches for geospatial data platforms, particularly around [[geonetwork]] and [[dcat-ontology]].
