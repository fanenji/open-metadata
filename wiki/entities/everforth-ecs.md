# Everforth ECS

Everforth ECS is a consulting firm that proposed a **Four-Category Maturity Model** for evaluating [[data-mesh]] readiness in government agencies. Their framework evaluates maturity across:

1. **Data as an Asset** — How data is valued, managed, and governed
2. **Self-Service Data** — How easily users can access and use data
3. **Data Governance** — Policies, standards, and compliance mechanisms
4. **Infrastructure** — Technical platform capabilities

The model is tailored to align with the Federal Data Strategy and is one of several frameworks synthesized in the [[data-mesh-maturity-assessment]].
