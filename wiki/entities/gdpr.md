# GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union regulation on data protection and privacy. It mandates specific requirements for handling personal data, including the right to be forgotten (deletion), the right to rectification (modification), and the use of techniques like pseudonymization. Compliance requires that data platforms support the ability to delete or modify PII when requested.
