# Simon Späti

Author of the article "Mastering Geospatial Analysis with DuckDB Spatial and MotherDuck" published on the MotherDuck blog. Simon Späti is a contributor to the DuckDB ecosystem, focusing on making geospatial analysis accessible to data engineers through practical demonstrations and clear explanations of spatial concepts.

## Contributions

- Authored a comprehensive beginner's guide to geospatial analysis with DuckDB Spatial
- Demonstrated practical use cases including chocolate store density analysis and store cluster detection using the Foursquare OS Places dataset
- Explained spatial indexing mechanics and performance optimization patterns
- Showcased MotherDuck's collaborative features for geospatial workloads
