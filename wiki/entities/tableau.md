# Tableau

Tableau is an enterprise business intelligence (BI) tool used for data exploration, visualization, and ad-hoc analysis. It is widely considered the "gold standard" for complex visualizations and remains a key component of the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]].

## Market Position

- **Adoption:** 38% (enterprise leader)
- **Trend:** Stable; held ground against new entrants
- **Strengths:** Complex visualization capabilities, enterprise contracts, high switching costs
- **Weaknesses:** Cost, slower innovation compared to newer tools

## Competitive Landscape

Tableau competes directly with [[Power BI]] (33%, growing via Microsoft 365 bundling) and [[Looker]] (22%, stable). In the broader BI ecosystem, it is often grouped with tools such as [[Mode]] and [[Metabase]], though these newer entrants have seen limited adoption. The "new breed" of BI tools (Mode, Sigma, Hex) have failed to disrupt Tableau's market position, with a combined adoption of less than 5%.
