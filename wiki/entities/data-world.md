# data.world

**data.world** is the organization responsible for the research and development of the benchmark evaluating the role of Knowledge Graphs in enhancing LLM accuracy for enterprise SQL databases. The research regarding the impact of Knowledge Graphs on LLM accuracy was conducted at this organization by authors Juan F. Sequeda, Dean Allemang, and Bryon Jacob.

The organization focuses on providing tools and methodologies for managing data context and semantic metadata to bridge the gap between raw data and AI-ready intelligence.
