# Rapportive

Rapportive was a social CRM startup that put social media profiles inside Gmail. It was founded by Martin Kleppmann and acquired by LinkedIn in 2012.

## Product

Rapportive was a browser extension that manipulated the Gmail web interface to show a social summary (profile picture, job title from LinkedIn, recent tweets, Facebook posts) next to emails. It helped users quickly identify and learn about people who emailed them.

## Tech Stack

Rapportive used a Rails app with a PostgreSQL database, Redis, and a custom graph database built on top of PostgreSQL.

## Acquisition

LinkedIn acquired Rapportive in 2012. The team continued working on a new product (LinkedIn Intro) before being disbanded. Kleppmann then moved to LinkedIn's stream processing team.
