# QuantStack

QuantStack is a software development company specializing in open-source scientific computing. They are a lead contributor to Project Jupyter and the consortium lead for the [[JupyterGIS]] project funded by the European Space Agency.

QuantStack has a long history of contributions to the Jupyter ecosystem and the broader open-source scientific computing community. They are also behind the [[JupyterCAD]] project.
