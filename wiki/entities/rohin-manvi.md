# Rohin Manvi

Lead author of the GeoLLM paper. Researcher at Stanford University (implied by affiliation context). Developed the GeoLLM method for extracting geospatial knowledge from large language models using auxiliary map data from OpenStreetMap.

## Contributions

- First author of "GeoLLM: Extracting Geospatial Knowledge from Large Language Models" (2023)
- Demonstrated that LLMs embed remarkable spatial information about locations
- Developed the novel method combining geographic coordinate prompts with OpenStreetMap data
- Showed 70% improvement over baselines and performance matching satellite-based benchmarks
