# Palo Alto Networks

Palo Alto Networks is a cybersecurity company that provided guidance on credential management for [[model-context-protocol]] (MCP) deployments. Their research highlighted the systematic poor credential management practices, including plaintext storage in configuration files and environment variables.

## Key Findings
- Documented plaintext credential storage in MCP configuration files
- Published "MCP Security Exposed: What You Need to Know Now"
- Their guidance informs [[mcp-security-best-practices]] for credential management
