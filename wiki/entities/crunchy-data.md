# Crunchy Data

Crunchy Data is the company behind [[Crunchy Bridge]], a managed PostgreSQL service. They specialize in deploying and managing PostgreSQL for a wide variety of use cases. The company developed the integration of [[DuckDB]] into Postgres via the extension framework to address analytical (OLAP) workloads natively within the Postgres ecosystem, avoiding the need for data replication to third-party analytics platforms.
