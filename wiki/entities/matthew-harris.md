# Matthew Harris

Author of the article "Reframing LLM 'Chat with Data': Introducing LLM-Assisted Data Recipes" published on Towards Data Science. Harris proposes the [[llm-assisted-data-recipes]] methodology, a two-stream architecture for conversational data analysis that separates recipe creation (powerful LLMs + human review) from recipe execution (cheap, deterministic). The work addresses limitations of existing [[chat-with-data-patterns]] and contributes to [[llm-data-analysis-cost-optimization]] strategies.
