# Soda

Soda is a data quality testing and monitoring tool that provides open-source and cloud-based solutions for validating data. It is frequently compared with [[Great Expectations]] and [[OpenMetadata]] in the data quality tooling landscape.

In the article "[[Simple, Easy, and Efficient Data Quality with OpenMetadata]]", Soda is used as a contrast to OpenMetadata's metadata-first approach. The article argues that Soda requires separate connection setup, metadata duplication, and technical expertise (Python/YAML), making it less accessible to non-technical users compared to OpenMetadata's no-code UI.

**Note:** These comparative claims come from a promotional blog post by OpenMetadata and should be treated as marketing assertions, not objective analysis.
