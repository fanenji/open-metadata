# Tushar Bhasin

Senior Data Engineer and Data Engineering Lead at [[BlaBlaCar]]. Co-presenter of the talk "One Thousands and One dbt Models: How BlaBlaCar Moved to dbt in 12 months" at Forward Data Conference 2024. Led the technical implementation of the dbt migration, including building the dbt DAG generator, query generator, and CLI setup tool.
