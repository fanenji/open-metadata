External freelance dbt expert hired by [[BlaBlaCar]] to accelerate their dbt migration. Provided informed decision-making, pair-of-fresh-eyes review of legacy pipelines, prototype building, and future problem scoping (e.g., the loss of BigQuery UI as a SQL development tool).
