# Bill Dollins

Bill Dollins is a geospatial developer and author of the GeoMusings blog. He explores modern geospatial workflows, particularly the use of [[DuckDB]] and [[GeoParquet]] as alternatives to traditional [[PostGIS]]-based pipelines.

His work focuses on bridging the gap between cloud-native geospatial formats and legacy systems. He demonstrated a pattern for using DuckDB as a proxy layer to convert GeoParquet data into [[GeoJSON]] using pure SQL, as a first step toward building a [[Koop]] provider that serves data through the [[GeoServices REST Specification]].

He attended [[FOSS4GNA]] where he learned about GeoParquet and DuckDB, leading to his experiments with these technologies.
