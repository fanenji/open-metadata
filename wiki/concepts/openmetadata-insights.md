# OpenMetadata Insights

OpenMetadata Insights provides analytics and reporting capabilities to help organizations understand and improve their data estate.

## Key Features

- **Data Asset Analytics:** Track usage and engagement with data assets.
- **App Usage Metrics:** Monitor how applications interact with the platform.
- **Coverage KPIs:** Measure the extent of metadata coverage across the data estate.
- **Ownership Dashboards:** Visualize data asset ownership and responsibilities.
- **Data Health Reports:** Assess the overall health and quality of data assets.
- **Governance Reports:** Track compliance with governance policies.

## Purpose

Insights enable data platform engineers, governance professionals, and business leaders to make data-driven decisions about their metadata management strategy, identify gaps in coverage, and monitor the effectiveness of governance initiatives.
