# Data Sovereignty Strategy

**Data Sovereignty** refers to the strategic capability of an organization (such as Regione Liguria) to maintain local control, autonomy, and legal authority over its data assets. 

In the context of the Data Platform, this strategy is implemented through:
- **On-Premise Deployment**: Keeping data within controlled, physical infrastructure to avoid reliance on external cloud providers.
- **Open-Source Software**: Utilizing technologies that prevent vendor lock-in and allow for full auditability of the software stack.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Aligning infrastructure with frameworks like **NIS2** and **DORA**, which necessitate robust, controlled, and auditable data environments.

By adopting an on-premise, open-source approach, the organization ensures that data management, security policies, and lifecycle processes remain under its direct jurisdiction.
