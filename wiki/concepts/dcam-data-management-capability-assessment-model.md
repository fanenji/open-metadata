# DCAM (Data Management Capability Assessment Model)

The **Data Management Capability Assessment Model (DCAM)** is a standard framework for evaluating data management and governance maturity. While not specific to [[data-mesh]], DCAM provides a foundation for understanding the [[federated-computational-governance]] dimension of a mesh assessment.

## Relevance to Data Mesh

- DCAM's governance maturity levels can be mapped to the governance dimension of a [[data-mesh-maturity-assessment]]
- Provides a structured approach to evaluating policy enforcement, compliance, and stewardship
- Referenced by Profisee as a complementary framework for mesh assessments

## Limitations

- DCAM predates data mesh and does not address domain ownership or data product thinking
- Focuses on centralized governance, which may conflict with mesh's decentralized model
- Requires significant effort to administer compared to lightweight approaches like the [[data-mesh-score]]
