# HVD Statistical Category

The statistical category of High-Value Datasets includes a wide range of economic and social statistics, primarily owned by ISTAT. Key datasets include:

- Industrial production, Producer Price Index (PPI), Sales volumes
- International trade statistics (exports/imports by partner, product, flow)
- Harmonized Consumer Price Index (HICP)
- National accounts (GDP aggregates, key indicators on corporations and households)
- Government expenditure and revenue, consolidated gross debt
- Environmental accounts and statistics
- Population, fertility, mortality
- Current healthcare expenditure
- Poverty and inequality
- Employment, unemployment, potential labor force
- European tourism flows

Data must be published in machine-readable formats (SDMX, CSV, etc.) with CC BY 4.0 license. Existing CC-BY 3.0 data must be upgraded.
