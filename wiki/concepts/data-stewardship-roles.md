# Data Stewardship Roles

**Data Stewardship** involves the assignment of specific roles and responsibilities to ensure data accountability, quality, and compliance within an organization.

## Core Responsibilities
*   **Ownership**: Defining who is responsible for the lifecycle of a specific dataset.
*   **Policy Enforcement**: Implementing rules for data access, usage, and retention.

*   **Auditability**: Ensuring that all changes to data (whether by humans or AI agents) are logged and traceable.
*   **Quality Oversight**: Acting as the final authority on resolving flagged anomalies identified by automated systems.

Automation of data quality checks requires a robust stewardship framework to ensure that when an AI agent flags an error, there is a clear process for remediation and accountability.
