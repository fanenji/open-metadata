# Data Engineer as Center of Excellence

A role definition from Maxime Beauchemin's 2017 manifesto describing data engineers as a "center of excellence" that defines standards, best practices, and certification processes for data objects. The team also leads education programs to help other teams become better citizens of the data warehouse.

## Responsibilities

- **Standards definition:** Establishing naming conventions, documentation requirements, and pipeline best practices.
- **Certification:** Defining certification processes for data objects, including service level agreements (SLAs) for core schemas.
- **Education:** Leading programs like Facebook's "Data Camp" or Airbnb's "Data University" to teach data proficiency across the organization.
- **Quality assurance:** Owning pockets of certified, high-quality areas in the data warehouse with clearly defined and measured SLAs.

## Examples

- **Airbnb:** A set of "core" schemas managed by the data engineering team with strict naming conventions, high-quality business metadata, and well-defined best practices.
- **Facebook:** A "data camp" education program where data engineers lead sessions teaching data proficiency.
