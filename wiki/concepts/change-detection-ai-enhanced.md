# Change Detection (AI-Enhanced)

AI-enhanced change detection uses deep learning models to identify changes in geospatial features over time by analyzing multi-temporal satellite and aerial imagery. This technique is critical for disaster response, environmental monitoring, urban planning, and climate research.

Tools like [[GeoAI]] integrate with [[torchange]] to provide change detection capabilities alongside other geospatial AI workflows.
