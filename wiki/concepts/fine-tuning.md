# Fine-tuning

Fine-tuning is the process of retraining an existing Large Language Model (LLM) on a specific, smaller dataset to adapt it to a particular task or domain.

## Comparison with RAG
While fine-tuning can improve model performance on specific tasks, it is often more resource-intensive and carries a higher risk of overfitting compared to [[retrieval-augmented-generation]].
