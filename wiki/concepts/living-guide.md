# Living Guide

A continuously updated resource that evolves through community contributions to remain relevant over time. The concept was applied to the [[dbt-canvas-guide]] created by [[Taylor Brownlow]] at [[Count.co]], which invites community members to submit suggestions and new resources for inclusion.

The living guide model addresses the challenge of documentation becoming outdated in fast-moving technical domains like dbt, where best practices and tooling evolve rapidly.
