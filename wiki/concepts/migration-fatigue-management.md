# Migration Fatigue Management

Organizational patterns used by [[BlaBlaCar]] to sustain a long (12+ month) migration project without burning out the team. Key tactics include:

- **Sprint Rotation:** Two teams of 3 engineers working in alternative sprints
- **Handover Rituals:** Structured knowledge transfer between rotating teams
- **Share-and-Learn Sessions:** Regular knowledge sharing
- **Automatic Reporting:** Reducing overhead by automating progress tracking and delay detection
- **Gamification:** Adding competitive elements to maintain engagement
- **Heavy Communication Investment:** Proactive sharing of learnings and issues

These patterns were part of the broader [[dbt-migration-strategy]] and were critical for maintaining momentum across the 18-month migration of 1,200 models.
