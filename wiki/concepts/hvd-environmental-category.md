# HVD Environmental Category (Earth Observation/Environment)

The environmental category of High-Value Datasets includes 26 INSPIRE themes covering earth observation and environmental data. Key datasets include:

- Hydrography, Protected Sites, Elevation, Geology, Land Cover, Orthoimagery
- Environmental Monitoring Facilities, Habitat and Biotopes, Land Use, Mineral Resources
- Natural Risk Zones, Oceanographic Features, Industrial Production, Soil, Species Distribution
- Air, Climate, Emissions, Nature Conservation, Noise, Waste, Water
- Energy Resources, Biogeographical Regions, Sea Regions, Area Management/Restriction/Regulation Zones

Data owners include ISPRA, regional environmental agencies (ARPA/APPA), and other specialized entities. Historical versions should be made available via API or bulk download where possible.
