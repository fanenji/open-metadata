# Data Observability

Data Observability is the practice of monitoring, alerting, and ensuring the health, accuracy, and reliability of data pipelines.

## Importance for GenAI
In the context of Generative AI, observability is essential for detecting "broken" inputs that lead to model hallucinations or incorrect outputs. It allows teams to proactively respond to incidents like failed jobs or misformatted data that put the integrity of the AI stack at risk.
