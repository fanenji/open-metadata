# Business Intelligence Tools

Business Intelligence (BI) tools allow business users to explore, drill down into, and visualize data, often without writing SQL. In the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]], popular BI tools include [[looker]], [[tableau]], [[mode]], and the open-source [[metabase]].

## Analytics and Collaboration

For data analysts and scientists who write SQL directly, analytics and collaboration tools like [[noteable]] and [[hex]] provide enhanced querying, visualization, and collaboration capabilities.

## Relationship to Wiki

This concept provides a consolidated overview of BI and analytics tools mentioned across the wiki, connecting them to the [[modern-data-stack-overview|Modern Data Stack]] architecture.
