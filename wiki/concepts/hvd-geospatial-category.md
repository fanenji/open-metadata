# HVD Geospatial Category

The geospatial category of High-Value Datasets includes seven datasets that must comply with INSPIRE data models and network services:

1. **Administrative Units** (ISTAT/IGM): National, regional, provincial, and municipal boundaries
2. **Geographic Names** (IGM): Place names and toponyms
3. **Addresses** (ANNCSU - ISTAT/Agenzia delle Entrate): Street names and civic numbers
4. **Buildings** (Multiple entities): Building footprints and characteristics
5. **Cadastral Parcels** (Agenzia delle Entrate): Land registry parcels
6. **Reference Parcels** (AGEA): Agricultural reference parcels for CAP
7. **Agricultural Parcels** (AGEA): Agricultural land parcels

Key requirements: INSPIRE-compliant data models, WMS/WFS network services, GeoPackage format, CC BY 4.0 license, metadata in RNDT.
