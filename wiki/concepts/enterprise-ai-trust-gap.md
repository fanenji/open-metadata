# Enterprise AI Trust Gap

The **Enterprise AI Trust Gap** is the lack of trust, governance, and explainability that blocks widespread adoption of AI in enterprise settings. Despite advances in generative AI, business leaders cite this as their #1 concern.

## Core Questions

When an AI system makes a recommendation, stakeholders need to ask:
- Why did the system recommend this action?
- How is this KPI calculated, and what sources are used?
- Can I audit or reproduce the results — across teams and over time?

## The Solution

A [[semantic-layer]] addresses the trust gap by providing:
- **Consistency**: One definition of KPIs across teams and tools.
- **Traceability**: Lineage from recommendation → metric → data source.
- **Explainability**: Agents can narrate why they took an action, with full lineage and context.
