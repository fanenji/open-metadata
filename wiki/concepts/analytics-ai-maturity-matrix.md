# Analytics × AI Data Maturity Matrix

A 2x2 diagnostic framework mapping organizational maturity on two independent axes: Analytics-Ready Data Maturity (horizontal) and AI-Ready Data Maturity (vertical). The matrix reveals that progress on one axis does not guarantee progress on the other.

## The Four Quadrants

### 1. Low Analytics × Low AI: The Fog
Data exists, but meaning doesn't. Reports are inconsistent, models are brittle, decisions rely on intuition. AI experiments feel magical when they work and inexplicable when they don't. Where most organizations start — and many stay.

### 2. High Analytics × Low AI: The Dashboard Trap
The most common and most dangerous quadrant. Dashboards are trusted, metrics are governed — but AI systems underperform. The organization has compressed away the context AI needs. See [[dashboard-trap]].

### 3. Low Analytics × High AI: The Black Box Lab
AI systems appear impressive in isolation, but humans don't trust them. Decisions can't be defended. Outputs can't be explained. Creates political resistance greater than technical failures.

### 4. High Analytics × High AI: The Coordinated System
The rarely achieved state. Analytics provides stable narratives about the past; AI reasons about the future. The two systems do not share pipelines, but they share truth. Humans trust the past while machines reason about the future.

## Key Insight

You don't move from analytics to AI. You mature **both independently** while keeping them aligned. The matrix is a diagnostic tool for identifying which quadrant an organization occupies and what path to pursue.
