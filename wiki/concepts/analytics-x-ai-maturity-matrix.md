# Analytics × AI Data Maturity Matrix

A 2x2 framework proposed by [[Animesh Kumar]] mapping organizational maturity across two orthogonal axes: Analytics Maturity (low/high) and AI Maturity (low/high). The matrix identifies four organizational states:

## The Fog (Low Analytics × Low AI)
Data exists but meaning doesn't. Reports are inconsistent, models are brittle, decisions rely on intuition. Most organizations start here.

## The Dashboard Trap (High Analytics × Low AI)
The most dangerous quadrant. Dashboards are trusted, metrics are governed, but AI systems underperform. The organization has compressed away the context AI needs. See [[dashboard-trap]].

## The Black Box Lab (Low Analytics × High AI)
AI demos work in isolation but humans don't trust them. Decisions can't be defended. The organization can't explain the present even as AI simulates the future.

## The Coordinated System (High Analytics × High AI)
The rarely achieved state. Analytics provides stable narratives about the past; AI reasons about the future. The two systems share truth but not pipelines.

## Key Principle
You don't move from analytics to AI. You mature both independently while keeping them aligned. This matrix is orthogonal to other maturity models like the [[data-maturity-model-for-sensitive-data]].
