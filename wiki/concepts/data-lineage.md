# Data Lineage

Data Lineage is the tracking of data from its original source through various transformations (e.g., conversion to embeddings) to its final use in a Large Language Model (LLM).

## Role in Troubleshooting
Lineage provides the transparency needed to troubleshoot AI errors and understand how models arrive at specific outputs. It allows engineers to trace the source of data as it is converted into embeddings for use in [[vector-databases]].
