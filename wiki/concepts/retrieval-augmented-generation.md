# Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a technique that incorporates external, dynamic datasets into the Large Language Model (LLM) generation process to provide context.

## Importance in Enterprise AI
RAG is currently considered the most practical path for enterprises to achieve "enterprise-ready" AI without the high cost and complexity of [[fine-tuning]]. It allows models to access up-to-date, company-specific information.

## Role of Data Engineering
Data engineers are responsible for the pipelines that feed accurate, updated, and optimized data into RAG systems, ensuring that the retrieval process is efficient and the data is high-quality.
