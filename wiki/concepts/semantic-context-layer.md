# Semantic Context Layer

The **Semantic Context Layer** represents the evolution of data catalogs from "standardizing metadata" to "standardizing meaning." 

## Purpose in the AI Stack
Traditional metadata (schemas and lineage) provides the structure of data but lacks the business intent. A semantic context layer provides the "reasoning framework" required by Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents. By defining typed relationships (e.g., "is equivalent to", "calculated from"), the layer prevents AI agents from "guessing" at relationships, thereby ensuring the reliability and trustworthiness of AI-generated insights.
