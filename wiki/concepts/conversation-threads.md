# Conversation Threads

Conversation Threads are threaded discussions initiated from data assets or tags within OpenMetadata. They enable users to ask questions, discuss details, and collaborate directly in the context of the data they are working with.

## Features

- **Threaded discussions:** Conversations are organized as threads, making it easy to follow the flow of discussion.
- **@mentions:** Users can mention other team members to bring them into the conversation.
- **Replies and reactions:** Participants can reply to messages and react with emojis.
- **Contextual:** Threads are attached to specific data assets (tables, dashboards, etc.) or tags, providing context for the discussion.

## Relationship to Other Features

- Conversation threads appear in the [[activity-feed|Activity Feed]], the central UI component for collaboration.
- They are one of the three pillars of [[data-collaboration|Data Collaboration]], alongside [[tasks|Tasks]] and [[announcements|Announcements]].
- Threads can be initiated around [[tags|Tags]] and [[glossary-terms|Glossary Terms]], enabling discussions about classification and governance.
