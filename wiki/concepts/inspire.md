# INSPIRE Directive

INSPIRE (Infrastructure for Spatial Information in the European Community) is an EU directive that aims to create a European Union spatial information infrastructure. It requires member states to document their geospatial datasets according to specific metadata standards (ISO 19115/19139).

## Relationship to Semantic Metadata

[[dcat-ontology|GeoDCAT-AP]] provides a bridge between INSPIRE compliance and the broader semantic web. A complete GeoDCAT-AP metadata record contains enough information to derive **INSPIRE compliance** — SEMIC provides XSLT transforms for this purpose. This enables organizations to maintain semantic metadata while still meeting INSPIRE requirements.
