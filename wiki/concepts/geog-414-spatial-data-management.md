# GEOG-414 Spatial Data Management

**GEOG-414** is a university course on Spatial Data Management offered at the University of Tennessee, authored and maintained by [[giswqs]]. The course covers the full lifecycle of geospatial data: storage, querying, analysis, and visualization, with an emphasis on modern, cloud-native tools and formats.

The course is delivered entirely through a public GitHub repository, exemplifying the [[course-as-code]] pattern. Materials include lecture slides, Jupyter notebooks, assignments, and datasets. Topics likely span [[postgis]] for spatial databases, [[duckdb-spatial-extension]] for in-process analytics, [[geoarrow]] and [[geoparquet]] for columnar geospatial storage, and [[iceberg-geospatial-support]] for lakehouse integration.

This resource provides a structured, university-level complement to the wiki's existing collection of tutorials and workshops (e.g., [[modern-gis-workshop]], [[cloud-native-geospatial-workflow]]).
