# Reverse ETL Consolidation

Reverse ETL consolidation is the predicted trend that reverse ETL will merge into ingestion tools (Fivetran, Airbyte) rather than remaining a standalone category.

## Current State

- Reverse ETL adoption slower than predicted: 22% vs 35% predicted
- 78% of companies don't use reverse ETL tools
- Census (8%) and Hightouch (6%) are the leaders
- Fivetran already entering with reverse ETL capability (3% adoption)

## Why Slower Growth

- Many teams built custom solutions (only ~10–20 API endpoints typical)
- Operational data often lives in app databases already
- Use case not universal (primarily B2B SaaS companies)
- Category awareness still building

## Prediction

The source predicts reverse ETL will consolidate into ingestion tools rather than remain a standalone category. This challenges the wiki's current treatment of [[reverse-etl-pattern]] as a separate pattern.
