# HVD Business/Company Ownership Category

The business and company ownership category of High-Value Datasets includes two datasets:

1. **Basic Company Information**: Key attributes including company name, legal form, registered address, VAT number, registration date, status
2. **Company Documents and Accounts**: Financial statements and annual accounts

Data owners include Chambers of Commerce (UnionCamere) and the Business Register. This category allows for additional conditions on reuse of personal data where necessary. CC BY 4.0 is the preferred license.
