# Glossary and Tags

Glossary and Tags are business metadata constructs in OpenMetadata used to classify and organize data assets. They enable data discovery and governance by providing a common vocabulary and categorization scheme.

## Glossary

A glossary defines a controlled vocabulary of business terms (e.g., "Customer ID", "Revenue") that can be associated with data assets. This helps bridge the gap between technical metadata and business understanding.

## Tags

Tags are flexible labels that can be applied to data assets for categorization, discovery, and governance purposes. They can be used to mark assets with sensitivity levels, data domains, or any other relevant attribute.

## Purpose

Together, glossaries and tags make it easier for users to find, understand, and govern their data assets within the [[OpenMetadata]] platform.
