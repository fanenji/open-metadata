# Vector Databases

Vector databases are a critical component of the modern AI stack, used to store and retrieve high-dimensional vector embeddings.

## Engineering Oversight
Data engineers play a vital role in managing these databases, ensuring that the data pipelines feeding the embeddings are reliable and that the retrieval processes are optimized for RAG workflows.
