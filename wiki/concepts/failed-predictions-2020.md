# Failed Predictions of 2020

This page documents predictions made in 2020 about the modern data stack that did not materialize by 2025.

## Wrong Predictions

1. **"Fivetran will be replaced by open-source"** — Fivetran still holds 34% market share.
2. **"BI will consolidate to 2–3 winners"** — BI remains fragmented with Tableau (38%), Power BI (33%), Looker (22%), and others.
3. **"DataOps will be a major category"** — Never materialized as a distinct category.
4. **"Everything moves to real-time"** — Batch processing still dominates (80%+ of workloads).
5. **"Data mesh will transform organizations"** — Minimal adoption (<5%).

## Lessons

Infrastructure predictions are hard. Established players are more resilient than expected. The source emphasizes that the "new breed" of tools (Mode, Sigma, Hex) failed to disrupt established BI tools, and real-time processing remains a minority approach despite years of predictions.
