# Domain Layer

The Domain Layer is one of the five [[lineage-layers]] in the OpenMetadata lineage view. It organizes datasets and assets into business-relevant categories such as "Ecommerce" or "Customer Data."

This layer provides contextual clarity and supports governance by aligning technical assets with business functions. It enables users to:
- View lineage filtered by business domain.
- Understand which data assets belong to which business area.
- Support data governance initiatives by linking technical metadata to business ownership.

The Domain Layer works in conjunction with [[classification-tags]] and [[glossary-terms]] to provide a rich business context for lineage exploration.
